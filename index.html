Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Coffee Fortune Reading app collects the following information:

  • Photos: Your coffee cup photos are used only for fortune reading and immediately deleted after processing
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through RevenueCat and Google Play Store

Note: We do not require or collect email addresses for registration.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered tasseography (coffee fortune reading) service
  • Personalize user experience
  • Provide technical support
  • Improve app performance

3. Data Security

We do not store any personal data permanently. Your coffee cup photos are processed for fortune reading and immediately deleted. No photos or personal information are kept on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses these third-party services:

  • OpenAI: AI service for fortune reading
  • RevenueCat: In-app purchase management
  • Google Play Store: Payment processing
  • Supabase: Data storage and authentication

5. Data Sharing

Your coffee cup photos are shared with OpenAI for AI-powered fortune reading analysis. Photos are immediately deleted after processing and are not stored by us or OpenAI. No other personal information is shared with third parties.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Object to data processing

7. Contact
